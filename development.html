    • Ripples

    This project explores poetic storytelling with a dynamic audio experience.

    Made in the Apple Developer Academy

    Solo project

    • Responsible for
    • Concept, code and assets
    • Sound design

    This project adds the structure of storytelling to an experiment with audio elements, chiming in and out dynamically depending on the "people" surrounding the protagonist, as it ponders about the everchanging nature of how the people we love come and go throughout our lives.

    My goal was to craft a story from my personal experiences, explore how the sounds of the protagonist and other characters would come together, and how this strategy would add as a storytelling feature.
